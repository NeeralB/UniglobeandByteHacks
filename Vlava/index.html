The Story

Depression is a recurring issue in the past history and even today during the coronavirus pandemic.

Every year around 5 to 7% of people in the world suffer from depression. Anyone can get depression, and the effects of depression are horrid: endless pain, torture, and maybe even suicide. Early depression symptoms are extremely hard to find; however multiple studies have shown factors that can cause depression and symbols that may indicate depression. Our website pieces all this information together to create one powerful webapp which is able to look for early symptoms of depression, answer any of your questions about the topic, and predict what your future may look like based on your current condition.
